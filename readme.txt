

Online Financial Literacy Courses Platform

Introduction

This project is an online platform designed to provide financial literacy courses to users. It aims to educate individuals on various financial topics such as budgeting, saving, investing, and managing debt. The platform offers a range of courses tailored to different skill levels and interests, providing users with the knowledge and skills needed to make informed financial decisions.

Features

1.User Authentication: Users can create an account, log in, and manage their profile.
2.Course Enrollment: Users can browse through available courses and enroll in the ones that interest them.
3.Course Content: Each course includes comprehensive content such as video lectures, reading materials, quizzes, and assignments.
4.Progress Tracking: Users can track their progress within each course, including completed lectures, quizzes, and assignments.
5.Discussion Forums: Users can participate in discussion forums to ask questions, share insights, and interact with other learners.
6.Certification: Upon successful completion of a course, users can receive a certificate of completion.
7.Admin Panel: Administrators can manage courses, user accounts, content, and forum discussions.

Technologies Used

1.Frontend: HTML, CSS, 
2.Backend: PHP, MySQL
3.Other Tool: Git, GitHub (for version control)

Usage

1.As a user, sign up for an account or log in if you already have one.
2.Browse through the available courses and enroll in the ones you're interested in.
3.Complete the course content, quizzes, and assignments at your own pace.
4.Engage in discussion forums to ask questions, share experiences, and connect with other learners.
5.Track your progress and earn certificates upon successful completion of courses.
